    • Open morning announcer positions to non-SU seniors, allowing more students to participate.

    We will create a webpage on our website that shows the current estimate of the prom ticket price. The thermometer will update as we have fundraisers, reflecting the progress towards the final ticket price.

    • Make the decoration of the class mnemonic cube a class-wide decision.
    • Invite seniors on stage to fill the mnemonic cube, creating a memorable tradition for the Class of '24.

    We want to bring back the significance of the mnemonic cube and engage seniors in its decoration.

  • Stuy Schedule App/Escalator Status

    The Stuy Schedule App is a SU IT project passed down from year to year. This year it was passed down to Anthony Chen who plans to add many features, such as a live escalator status. The Chen-Karim ticket is the only ticket that can promise maintenance and new features.

  • StuyCompare.Live
    • Helped over 800 students this year find others in their classes

    We will continue to develop StuyCompare.Live to facilitate schedule sharing and help students find others in their classes. Our goal is to provide a seamless and efficient platform for student interactions.

  • Grad Package Options
    • Offer the option to purchase the Senior Grad Package without the cap and gown for those who don't need or want it.
    • Introduce more varieties of merch, such as bracelets and beanies, to enhance the Class of '24 merchandise options.

    We want to provide flexibility and a wider range of choices in the Senior Grad Package.

  • Paint the Senior Bar

    We propose repainting the Senior Bar as a way for the Class of '24 to leave a lasting mark on the school and showcase our class spirit.

  • Book Drive
    • Organize a book drive where people can donate their old books.
    • All remaining books at the end of the fundraiser will be donated to libraries and children's shelters.

    We aim to promote literacy and support local communities through the book drive initiative.

  • Online Events
    • Continue hosting online events such as game pigeon tournaments and movie nights to provide opportunities for seniors to destress and connect.

    We believe in the value of online events and their positive impact on student well-being.

  • Streamlined Prom Ticket Buying Process

    We will work on simplifying and improving the process of purchasing prom tickets to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience for all seniors.

  • Improved Collaboration with the Indicator

    We will establish a better relationship with the Stuyvesant Indicator, working together to promote Senior Spirit Days and other important initiatives. Together, we can enhance the student experience and school spirit.

  • Yearbook Theme
    • If elected, the yearbook theme will be Taylor Swift-themed.
    • For those who may not be fans of Taylor Swift, we will ensure alternative options and themes for the yearbook.
